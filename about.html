About Experience Points

Our Mission

Experience Points is a professional productivity and goal-tracking platform designed to help individuals and teams monitor their progress in various skills and financial goals. Our mission is to empower users to quantify their personal and professional growth through data-driven insights.

The Platform

Our platform offers a comprehensive solution for tracking various types of goals:

  • Activity Hours: Monitor time spent on activities like Tennis, BJJ, Cycling, Skiing, and more
  • Financial Goals: Track progress on important financial milestones such as debt repayment
  • Professional Skills: Measure improvement in work-related competencies

Data Security

Experience Points prioritizes the security and privacy of user data. Our platform implements industry-standard security measures including secure SSL encryption, regular security audits, and strict data access controls. For more information about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Corporate Use

Experience Points is designed for both individual and enterprise use. Our platform is compatible with corporate environments and complies with standard security protocols required by most organizations.
